Resume

 

I am an undergraduate student at The University of Texas - Rio Grande Valley, currently majoring in Computer Science and doing a minor in Graphic Design. I've been a Research Assistant at the Social Systems Laboratory under the supervision of Dr. Megan Strait. I've worked with C, C++ and Java to implement algorithms and produce mobile applications, as well as HTML, CSS, and Ruby to create web applications. I am also proficient in Adobe software programs Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign.

My research interests are in human-computer interaction; in particular, I have worked in researching antisocial behavior in human-robot interactions, specifically abusive behavior towards highly humanlike, female-gendered, racialized robots.
