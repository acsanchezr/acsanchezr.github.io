I got my bachelor's in Computer Science and minor in Graphic Design from The University of Texas - Rio Grande Valley. I am now pursuing a master's in Sociology at the same institution, as my interests are in Human-Computer Interaction, particularly in the societal impact of technologies and in how people utilize technologies for social good.
I'm also a Research Assistant at the Social Systems Laboratory under the supervision of Dr. Megan Strait, where I've contributed in investigating antisocial behavior in human-robot interactions, specifically abusive behavior towards highly humanlike, female-gendered, racialized robots.

As of now I work as a Graduate Assistant at the Office of Technology Commercialization at UTRGV, where I perform several tasks, among which are the design of documents and visuals for events. I also create visuals as the design lead of the DSC club at UTRGV.

You can access my Github, my design portfolio, and my Medium blog.
