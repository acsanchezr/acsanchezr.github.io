Hey there! I'm Cecy :)

I have a bachelors in computer science and a minor in graphic design, and am currently doing a masters in sociology. I'm interested in topics regarding human-computer interaction, specially in understanding the role of technologies in society, and in how society influences and shapes the design and development of tech. I'm a research assistant at the Social Systems laboratory at UTRGV, where I work under the supervision of Dr. Megan Strait in investigating antisocial behavior in human-robot interactions, and a grad assistant at the Office of Technology Commercialization. I'm also the design lead at my school's Developer Student Club, where I'm in charge of creating graphics for upcoming events that are posted on our social media platforms.

Some of the things that I do during my free time and/or as personal projects are writing about tech and society (you can check out my blog!), reading crime novels and works of non-fiction (usually related to sociology or HCI), and painting .
I love dogs, music in spanish, and moccha frappuccinos <3
