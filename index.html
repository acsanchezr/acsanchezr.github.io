Hey there! I'm Cecy :) I did my bachelors in computer science and a minor in graphic design, and am currently doing a masters in sociology. I'm interested in topics regarding Human-Computer Interaction, specially in understanding the role of technologies in society, and in how society influences and shapes the design and development of tech. I'm a research assistant at the Social Systems laboratory at UTRGV, where I work under the supervision of Dr. Megan Strait in investigating antisocial behavior in human-robot interactions, and am also a grad assistant at the Office of Technology Commercialization. I'm also the design lead of my school's Developer Student Club.

I like to write about tech and society (you can check out my blog!), read crime novels and works of non-fiction (recently been into feminism and literature concerning colonialism), paint , and moccha frapps.
