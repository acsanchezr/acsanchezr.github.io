I obtained my Bachelor's in Computer Science from The University of Texas - Rio Grande Valley. I also hold a minor in Graphic Design, and am currently pursuing a Master's in Sociology at the same institution. I've been a Research Assistant at the Social Systems Laboratory under the supervision of Dr. Megan Strait since 2017.

My research interests are in human-computer interaction; in particular, I've worked in researching antisocial behavior in human-robot interactions, specifically abusive behavior towards highly humanlike, female-gendered, racialized robots.
